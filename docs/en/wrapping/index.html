Frequently asked questions

  • How does one-way vision (perforated) film work?

    Perforated vinyl film has micro-punctures over the entire surface. We print on one side, and the human eye is drawn to the bright, printed graphic, ignoring the holes. From the inside, the black adhesive layer and the holes allow you to see through the window clearly.

  • Is the window film easily removable?

    Yes. Professional window films can be removed cleanly without leaving residue or damaging the glass. This makes them ideal for temporary promotions or updating office branding.

  • How should I clean my wrapped windows?

    Clean the film with a soft cloth or sponge and a mild soap-and-water solution. Avoid using abrasive cleaners, ammonia-based products, or sharp objects near the film's surface and edges.
