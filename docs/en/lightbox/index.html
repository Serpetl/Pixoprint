Frequently asked questions

  • What are the benefits of using LED illumination?

    LEDs are the modern standard for lightboxes. They are significantly more energy-efficient, have a much longer lifespan (up to 50,000+ hours), provide brighter and more even illumination, and are more durable than traditional fluorescent tubes.

  • Can the graphics on my lightbox be changed?

    Absolutely. Most lightboxes are designed with easily swappable graphic faces. This makes it cost-effective to update your branding, run promotions, or change menu items without replacing the entire sign structure.

  • Do you handle the installation?

    Yes, we offer a complete service that includes professional installation by our experienced team. We ensure your sign is mounted securely and all electrical connections are safe and up to code. We can also prepare the sign for you if you have your own installers.
