How It works

  1. Prepare your 2D/3D design file and submit it using the contact form

  3. Pick up your finished parts or let us arrange a delivery

Frequently asked questions

  • What file formats do you accept?

    We accept 2D vector files (DXF, AI, SVG) and 3D models (STEP, IGES, F3D). If you have another format, please contact us.

  • What is the typical lead time?

    Lead times vary depending on the project complexity and current workload. For standard orders, we aim for 3-5 business days. We will provide an estimated completion time with your quote.

  • Do you offer design assistance?

    Yes, we offer basic design assistance and can help optimize your files for CNC milling. For more complex design needs, we can refer you to our design partners.
