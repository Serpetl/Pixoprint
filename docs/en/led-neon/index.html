Custom LED Neon Signs

The classic neon vibe, reinvented with modern, durable technology.

The Future of Neon is Here

Our LED neon signs are crafted using state-of-the-art technology. We start with a high-quality acrylic base (6mm or thicker) and use a precision CNC router to mill custom channels for your design. We then embed vibrant LED strips into these channels and cover them with flexible, light-diffusing silicone tubes. The result is a stunning, flawless sign with the classic neon glow, but with none of the drawbacks of traditional glass neon.

Why Choose Our LED Neon?

Durable & Safe

Made from acrylic and flexible silicone, our signs are shatter-proof. They run on low 12V voltage, making them safe to touch.

Energy Efficient

LED technology consumes up to 80% less energy than traditional neon and has a lifespan of over 50,000 hours.

Vibrant & Versatile

With a huge range of colors for both the light and the silicone tubing, the creative possibilities are endless.

    How Your Sign is Made

    1. Design & Approval

    We take your idea or logo and create a digital proof for your approval before production begins.

    2. Precision Milling

    We use a CNC machine to perfectly mill the design channels into the thick acrylic backboard.

    3. LED & Silicone

    Our craftsmen carefully install the LED strips and fit the colored silicone tubes into the channels.

    4. Assembly & Testing

    We wire the sign, attach the power supply, and rigorously test it to ensure a perfect, even glow.
