What is LED Neon?

LED neon is a modern, flexible, and safe alternative to traditional glass neon. It uses energy-efficient LEDs encased in a durable silicone shell, allowing for intricate designs and vibrant colors while being safer and more durable.

Why Choose LED Neon?

Durable & Safe

Made from flexible silicone, our signs are shatterproof and cool to the touch.

Energy Efficient

Uses low-voltage LEDs, consuming significantly less power than glass neon.

Fully Customizable

Endless possibilities with various colors, fonts, and sizes to match your brand.

    How It's Made

    Design & CNC

    We convert your design into a path for our CNC machine, which then carves a groove into a clear acrylic backplate.

    Silicone Shaping

    Flexible silicone tubing is carefully shaped and pressed into the groove, perfectly matching your design.

    LED Installation

    High-density LED strips are inserted into the silicone tubing, ensuring bright and even illumination.

    Wiring & Testing

    We complete the wiring and rigorously test every sign to ensure it meets our quality standards.
