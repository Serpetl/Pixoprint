Frequently asked questions

  • Will the wrap damage my car's paint?

    No, quite the opposite. A high-quality vinyl wrap professionally applied to factory paint will protect it from minor scratches, UV exposure, and road debris. It can be removed safely without damaging the paint underneath.

  • How long does a vehicle wrap last?

    Depending on the film quality and environmental factors, a professional wrap can last between 5 to 7 years. We use premium materials to ensure the best possible longevity and finish.

  • How do I care for my wrapped vehicle?

    We recommend hand washing with a mild automotive soap. Avoid automatic car washes with harsh brushes and pressure washers, as they can damage the film. We will provide you with detailed care instructions.
